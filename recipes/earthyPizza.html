Earthy Pizza

Description

Veggies galore. This wonderful pizza delivers all the flavors from a salad to a delicious pizza. This is not your average greasy, crusty, spiced pizza. What we have here is a pizza that relies on the fresh flavors of ventables, as if it were grown straight from the garden. When cooked on the grill a fabulous chared crust will leave you wanting more than one slice.

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Vodka sause
  • Pesto
  • Spinach
  • Fresh basil
  • Mushrooms
  • Red onions
  • Peppers
  • Salami
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Slice peppers, mushrooms, and onions into medium slices
  2. Mix together vodka sause and pesto
  3. Cover crust in the sause
  4. Arrange spinach and fresh basil until all sause is covered
  5. Lay out salami until spinach is covered
  6. Arrange peppers, mushrooms, and onions in an even layer upon the salami
  7. Lay out mozzarella cheese slices, be sure to fill the center. Don't worry about gaps, when it melts they will fill in
  8. Sprinkle shredded parmesan cheese over the whole pizza
  9. Bake in oven, or grill, at 400 degrees F or until crust is firm and cheese is lightly browned
