Chicken Avacado Spread

Description

A delicious spread for crakers or chips. It can even be made into a sandwich spread.

Ingredients

  • Avacado
  • Bonless skinless chicken
  • Red onion
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder

Steps

  1. Slice avacado in half and remove pit
  2. Remove avacado from the scin and smash until there are no mor large chunks
  3. Cook chicken with desired seasoning, first sear then cover and cook on low until fully cooked
  4. Chop chicken in to bite sized chunkks
  5. In smashed avacado add onion poweder, garlic powder chopped cleery, chopped onions, and chopped chicken
  6. Stir until all ingredients are fully mixed
